					goto security
				`		"Am I in trouble?"`

			label security

				goto toearth
			label notrouble
			`"On the contrary."`
				goto toearth
			label toearth

            `Please visit our headquarters on next time you come to Earth. We have a suite of highly profitable investments for customers of your caliber."`

			`Your terminal beeps with a message from Goulding.`
			`"Transport has been arranged, please head to the spaceport."`


mission "Galactic Capital Intro B"
	invisible
	source "Earth"
	to offer
		has "Galactic Capital Intro A: done"
		"credits" > 1000000
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "scene/gci_galactic_capital"
			`Fitting the largest bank in human space, GCI headquarters is a towering pyramid of glass and polished stone. You are escorted to Warren Goulding's office.`
            `"Let's get to business. We offer Private Market investments to our premium clients. These are high growth initiatives not listed on the Galactic Stock Exchange.`
			`To make a long story short, our investment team does the hard work using seed capital, investors collect a risk free salary after fees."`
			choice
				`	(Nod)`
				goto nod
			label nod
			`"Before I present the offer, standard compliance requires your biometric signature on this Non-Disclosure Agreement, a breach results in an immediate asset freeze and cordial memos from our elite legal team."`
			`You sign.`
			`"For your onboarding, we have a vacancy in a Series A venture debt facility for Sapphire Biotech. Xenopathogen research.`
			`Investment:	1,000,000 credits`
			`Daily yield:	2,500 credits (after fees)`
			` "This serves as a preliminary alignment of our interests. Do you authorize the capital transfer?"`
			choice
				`	(Sign the contract.)`
					goto money
				`	(Decline.)`
					goto nomoney
			label money
			`"Confirmed. GCI values partners who act with conviction.`
			`Private investments will now regularly appear in your job board, we will also contact you when our primary acquisitions require your profile."`
				accept
			label nomoney
			`"A conservative choice. We will still notify you of investment opportunities via the job board. Your success is our success, Captain <last>"`
				decline
	on accept
		payment -1000000
		"salary: Galactic Capital" += 2500
		set "global: gcijobs active"
	on daily
		fail
